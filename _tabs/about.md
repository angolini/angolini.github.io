I'm Daiane Angolini, Engineer, Senior Staff at Qualcomm, where I work with
embedded Linux and the Yocto/OpenEmbedded ecosystem. I also contribute actively
to [Foundries.io](https://foundries.io)'s Linux microPlatform (LmP), including
coordinating recent releases.

Previously, I spent over five years at Foundries.io and before that eleven years
at Freescale Semiconductor/NXP, working on BSPs, board customizations, Android,
and Yocto-based platforms for i.MX hardware.

## Books

I'm a published author on the Yocto Project:

- **[Embedded Linux Development with Yocto Project](https://bit.ly/first-yocto-book)**
  — 1st, 2nd, and 3rd editions (Packt Publishing), co-authored with Otávio Salvador
- **Heading for the Yocto Project** (Kindle), co-authored with Otávio Salvador
- **Embedded Linux Development with Yocto Project** — 4th edition (Packt Publishing,
  forthcoming), covering Yocto Project 6.0 "Wrynose" LTS

## What I write about here

Practical notes on things I learn, debug, or figure out the hard way:
